About

Easily click through restaurants around you

Traveling to a new city and need to find restaurants near you? Out for a night on the town and have the late night munchies? Maybe you just want to explore your neighborhood for something new. Regardless, Swipe Bite makes exploring new restaurants near you easy and fun.

Swipe Bite gets all restaurant information and photos from Google Places and can pull your location (with permission) directly from your phone making it hassle free. By clicking on the map icon, you are immeditiately taken to Google Maps and can make your way to the restaurant




Features

Easily click through restaurants around you

  • Tap left and right to find restaurants near you.
  • The name, reviews and the location will be provided by tapping the menu bar on the right bottom corner of the image.
  • In settings, change your desired distance.
  • Save restaurants for later and go back and find it in your saved favorites page.
